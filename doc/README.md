# Tomb

Tomb is a narrative style game with survival mechanics about an astronaut stuck in a space station orbiting the Earth. Why they are stuck? It is because that on the Earth below humanity is barely surviving a new threat that seems poised to wipe them all out.

You can't see any of this, only view Earth from your vantage point above and listen to people through your on station radio, having to take into account your position in orbit and where the signals are coming from to listen in on them and actually talk to someone.

You are not in a good situation yourself. Unable to return to Earth's surface, all you can do is stretch your supplies out, figure out how to make more, and keep your machines running for as long as possible. Though you know that it will be losing battle for you.
